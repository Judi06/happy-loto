2. Purpose of the Platform

Fair Solidarity is an equitable donation platform that allows eligible users to receive, through a random draw, goods or services that can improve their daily lives (household equipment, utility vehicles, professional training, etc.).

3. Eligibility

3.1 Participation Criteria

To participate in our donation system, you must:

  • Be at least 18 years old
  • Reside in a geographic area covered by our services
  • Provide the required supporting documents
  • Not have already benefited from a donation through our Platform in the last 12 months

3.2 Required Documents

To validate your participation, you will need to provide:

  • A valid ID
  • Proof of address less than 3 months old
  • Depending on the allocated donation, additional documents may be requested

4. Allocation Process

Donations are allocated according to the following process:

  • Participation via the random draw wheel
  • Verification of provided information
  • Delivery of the donation within 30 working days after validation

5. User Commitments

By using the Platform, you agree to:

  • Not resell the allocated donation within 2 years
