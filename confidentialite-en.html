Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2021

1. Introduction

Solidarité Équitable ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and French legislation.

2. Data Collected

2.1 Types of data

We may collect the following categories of personal data:

Category Examples Purpose
Identity Name, surname, date of birth, ID document Identity verification, eligibility
Contact Address, email, phone number Communication, donation delivery
Technical data IP address, browser type, interaction history Security, service improvement
Supporting documents Proof of address, professional documents Eligibility criteria validation

2.2 Collection methods

We collect data when you:

  • Participate in the prize wheel
  • Complete our prize claim form
  • Upload supporting documents
  • Communicate with our customer service
  • Browse our platform (cookies)

3. Processing Purposes

Your data is processed for the following purposes:

  • Donation management: Eligibility verification, prize allocation and delivery
  • Customer relations: Responding to requests, important notifications
  • Service improvement: Usage analysis, new feature development
  • Legal obligations: Compliance with current regulations

4. Legal Bases for Processing

Our processing is based on:

  • Contract performance: To provide the requested service
  • Legal obligation: To meet our regulatory requirements
  • Legitimate interest: To improve our services and prevent fraud
  • Consent: For non-essential cookies and marketing communications

5. Data Sharing

5.1 Recipients

Your data may be shared with:

  • Service providers: Delivery, hosting, technical support (under strict contract)
  • Public authorities: Only upon legal request
  • Donation partners: For specific prize delivery (minimum required data)

5.2 Transfers outside EU

Your data is hosted in the European Union. In case of transfer outside the EU, we would implement appropriate safeguards (EU Standard Contractual Clauses).

6. Retention Period

We retain your data:

  • Active data: 3 years after last interaction for non-recipients
  • Recipients: 5 years after receiving donation (legal obligation)
  • Supporting documents: 1 year after verification
  • Anonymized data: Kept for statistical purposes

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Strict access control
  • Regular security audits
  • Staff training

8. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: Obtain a copy of your data
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion under certain conditions
  • Right to restriction: Restrict processing
  • Right to object: Object to processing for legitimate reasons
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in structured format

8.1 Exercising your rights

To exercise your rights or ask questions about this policy:

Email: dpo@solidarite-equitable.org

Mail: DPO - Solidarité Équitable, 12 Rue de la Solidarité, 75000 Paris

We will respond within one month. In case of refusal, we will explain our decision.

9. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use:

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for platform operation
  • Analytical cookies: To measure audience (with your consent)

You can manage your preferences via our cookie banner or browser settings.

10. Policy Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be effective upon publication on our platform.

11. Contact and Complaints

For any questions or complaints regarding your data protection:

Data Protection Officer: dpo@solidarite-equitable.org

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the CNIL (www.cnil.fr).
